Escape rooms 101

-Escape Room basics-

If you are a complete beginner and are currently in Ireland you might have looked up 'fun activities to do near me' and escape rooms popped up - ohhh how exciting, that looks fun how does this all work and where is the best escape rooms near me? Before you check out the many amazing escape rooms on our 'Rooms' page lets talk a bit about the basics of an escape room for you newbies (If you are a seasoned escape room goer you might want to click on the 'Rooms' tab and it will bring you to the juicy best escape rooms in Ireland)

-So, what exactly is an escape room?-

An escape room, is a themed room where players are locked in and have 60 minutes to escape. Themes can vary from prison cells to haunted mansions to luxury train stations any theme is possible in an escape room which makes every new escape room experience different and unique. Players escape by solving puzlles and tasks. Each puzzle you solve you will get a clue to solve the next one and eventually it will lead to you escaping the room.

-Essential tips for first timers-

Choose your best team ! You are allowed up to eight people in most escape rooms but I recommened no more then four. The more people the more chaotic it can get. The ideal amount of people per escape room is between two to four.

Listen carefully to your gamemaster (The gamemaster is who will bring you into the game - they are watching over your game and can assist you if you get stuck) At the beggining of every escape game the Gamemaster will go over a breefing on the room and the back story behind the room. Listen carefully for early clues.

Communication is the most important tip - Constantly talk and let people know what clues you have found, who knows mabye they are looking for the exact item you picked up!

-Conclusion-

Escape rooms are not like anything else, enjoy them and most importanly have fun. They are great for all ages and all levels of problem-solving. Remember you dont have to be Albert Eninstein to solve the puzzles but you might feel like him once you have escaped !!
